Story

For years, I would distribute software manually. I’d get a payment and then email the software, either from my desktop or from my Blackberry. Why didn’t I use Zen Cart? I know, it seems crazy. Honestly, I was just too busy building sites for other people to build my own.

My friend David Croy, and later, a client named Adrian Frankel encouraged me to set up an online store, and finally I did.
